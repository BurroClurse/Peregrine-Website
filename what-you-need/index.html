What you need

The kit for a session.

Peregrine turns a laser training cartridge, a printed target, and your iPhone into a scored dry-fire session. Most of the setup is familiar equipment you may already own.

Required

An unloaded handgun

Use the handgun you train with, unloaded, with a matching laser training cartridge. Keep live ammunition out of the room and follow the maker’s dry-fire guidance.

Required

A red laser training cartridge

Choose a red laser training cartridge in the correct caliber. It flashes on the target when the striker drops, and that flash is what Peregrine scores.

Required

A printed Peregrine target

Print a target from the app on standard letter paper. Keep the QR code and black corner markers sharp, flat, and uncovered; black-and-white printing is fine.

Required

An iPhone with Peregrine

Peregrine uses the iPhone camera to find the target and detect laser hits. Allow camera access when the app asks so it can start a session.

Recommended

A stable phone stand

A stable phone stand or adjustable tripod holds the camera near target height, keeps the phone still, and makes framing easier.

Nice to have

Reset gear

A TRT dry-fire insert, dry-fire magazine, or trigger-reset device can reduce the need to rack the slide between repetitions.
